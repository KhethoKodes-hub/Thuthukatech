The story of Thuthuka Tech begins with a vision to build technology that truly serves people — starting with our founder's own journey from technician to full-stack developer.

Meet The Founder

Khetho Mngomezulu

Full-Stack Developer with a passion for building scalable, user-focused applications that solve real-world problems.

"Thuthuka — meaning 'to rise' in isiZulu — it isn't just a name. It's my promise. I started this company to help businesses elevate through technology that works, scales, and delivers real value."

With hands-on experience across the full development stack — from React and Node.js to cloud deployment and database architecture — Khetho has built production-ready systems for major organisations including Rand Water, ABSA, and Anglo American. His journey from IT technician to lead developer gives him a unique understanding of both infrastructure and code, bridging the gap between technical excellence and business outcomes.
