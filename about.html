Our Journey

The Thuthuka Story

From a bold idea to a trusted technology partner — our journey of rising and elevating.

2017
Founded in Johannesburg
Thuthuka Tech was born from a vision to bring enterprise-grade technology to South African businesses.

Team Expansion & Cloud Focus
